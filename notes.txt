## ✅ **Step-by-Step Power BI Dashboard Guide (Financial Insights)**

---

### 🧩 **STEP 1: Import Clean Data**

1. **Open Power BI Desktop**
2. Click on **“Home > Get Data > Text/CSV”**
3. Select your cleaned file: `Cleaned_Financial_Sample.csv`
4. Click **Load**
   ➤ Data will now be available as a table.

---

### 🧭 **STEP 2: Data Model & Date Column Settings**

1. Go to **Data View**
2. Click on the `Date` column
3. Make sure it's **Data Type = Date**
4. Create a **hierarchy** (Right click → Create Hierarchy) to include:

   * Year
   * Month
   * Day (optional)

This will help you in time-series visualizations.

---

### 📊 **STEP 3: Create KPI Cards (Top Summary)**

From **Report View**:

1. Insert **Card Visual**

2. Drag following fields one by one:

   * `Sales` → Sum → Rename to "Total Sales"
   * `Profit` → Sum → Rename to "Total Profit"
   * `Discounts` → Average → Rename to "Avg Discount"
   * Create new DAX Measure for **Profit Margin**:

     ```DAX
     Profit_Margin = DIVIDE(SUM([Profit]), SUM([Sales]))
     ```

3. Format card titles and use ₹ or \$ if needed.

---

### 📅 **STEP 4: Add Line Chart for Monthly Trend**

1. Insert **Line Chart**
2. Axis: `Date` (Hierarchy → Month, Year)
3. Values: `Sales`, `Profit`
4. Add slicer for `Year` to compare 2 years
5. Format with proper colors (e.g. blue for sales, green for profit)

---

### 🌍 **STEP 5: Add Bar Chart for Country Wise Profit**

1. Insert **Stacked Bar Chart**
2. Axis: `Country`
3. Value: `Profit`
4. Sort by descending profit

---

### 📦 **STEP 6: Add TreeMap or Pie Chart for Segment-wise Sales**

1. Insert **TreeMap**
2. Group: `Segment`
3. Value: `Sales`

Or you can use Pie/Donut for similar view.

---

### 📈 **STEP 7: Bar Chart for Top Products by Profit**

1. Insert **Clustered Column Chart**
2. Axis: `Product`
3. Value: `Profit`
4. Top N filter: Show Top 10 by Profit

---

### 📉 **STEP 8: Discount Impact Chart**

1. Insert **Scatter Chart** or **Line Chart**
2. Axis: `Discounts`
3. Value: `Profit`
4. See if more discount leads to more/less profit

---

### 📋 **STEP 9: Add Detailed Table**

1. Insert **Table**
2. Add columns: `Date`, `Product`, `Country`, `Segment`, `Sales`, `Profit`, `Discounts`

---

### 🎛 **STEP 10: Add Slicers (Filters)**

1. Insert **Slicer Visuals**
2. Add these fields:

   * `Year`
   * `Country`
   * `Segment`
   * `Product`

Users can filter dashboard based on these.

---

## 🖌 **Final Touch: Formatting**

* Use **theme colors** from “View > Themes”
* Use **custom titles** for each visual
* Adjust visuals to make layout clean and balanced
